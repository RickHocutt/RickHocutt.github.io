About Me

I have twenty years of graphic design experience and I've spent the past ten years designing and building websites that conform to current web standards. I have a strong background in User Experience and User Interface Design and hand code valid HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My recent duties as a UX Designer for Alight Solutions, Aon, Cardinal Health and AutoZone include determining user flows and creating wireframes, prototypes and design comps and converting them into production ready assets. On my own time I enjoy experimenting with different ways to build sites including Jekyll, Panini, Angular and WordPress.

This site is hosted on GitHub, want to see the code? Check out the repo at: https://github.com/RickHocutt/RickHocutt.github.io.
